Hello,They are Enhypen!

ENHYPEN is a seven-member South Korean boys group that debuted in November 2020 under Belift Lab. The group consists of members Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki.

The name ENHYPEN means connection, discovery and growth. Similar to how a hyphen connects words to discover a new meaning. ENHYPEN refers to how the members discovered each other, are connected, and will continue to grow together.⋆୨♡୧⋆
